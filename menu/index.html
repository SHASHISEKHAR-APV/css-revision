Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • MILK
  • Oil

Recipe

  1. MAKE SYRUP OF IT .
  2. MAKE THE MILK LIKE PANEER.
  3. THEN DICE ASMALL ROUND STRUCTURE .
  4. FRY IT IN OIL.
  5. NOW DIP IT IN THE SYRUP.
  6. NOW LEAV IT FOR FEW HOURS
  7. GLUB JAMMUN IS NOW READY TO SERVE
